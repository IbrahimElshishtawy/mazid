## About

Factory Management Dashboard is a modern management platform created for factories that need a smarter and more structured way to manage their daily business operations.

It is designed for manufacturing environments where visibility, organization, and productivity tracking are essential for growth.

---

## Main Capabilities

- Workforce and role management
- Attendance tracking
- Task assignment and follow-up
- Production monitoring
- Inventory control
- Reports and analytics
- Business operations support
